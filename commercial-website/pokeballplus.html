Poke Ball Plus

The Poke Ball Plus is is a device shaped like a Poke Ball that can connect to Pokemon: Let's Go, Pikachu! and Let's Go, Eevee!, Pokemon GO, and Pokemon Sword and Shield. It includes 2 buttons: a Top Button and a Control Stick in the center, which acts as both a button and a joystick. It also has motion control capabilities.
